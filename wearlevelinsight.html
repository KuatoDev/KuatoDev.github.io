Wear Level Insight

verified Version 4.0.0 android Android 12+ vpn_key Root Required

Introducing "Wear Level Insight" - the Android app designed to check the health of your device's storage. Easily detect potential issues with your storage (UFS/eMMC) ensuring that your device runs smoothly.

warning Disclaimer:
We are not responsible for bricked devices, missing recovery partitions, dead microSD cards, runaway pets, nuclear wars, or you getting fired because you flashed ABL partitions. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK!

settings Requirements & Compatibility

  • check_circle Root Access: Required to scan storage partitions.
  • check_circle Storage Types: UFS 1.0 up to 4.0 (Functionality depends on OEM Kernel).
  • check_circle Project Status: Most Android devices will work, but some edge cases may vary.
