WINCross

Cross-Platform Windows Control (formerly WoA Helper)

smartphone Android 12+ vpn_key Root Required layers Dual Boot

WINCross is a powerful utility designed to manage dual-boot environments between Windows and Android directly from your Android device. It allows you to perform critical maintenance, flash UEFI, and optimize Windows without needing to reboot.

warning Disclaimer:
We are not responsible for bricked devices, missing recovery partitions, dead microSD cards, runaway pets, or nuclear wars. Flashing UEFI or modifying partitions is risky. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK!

stars Key Features

  • check_circle Mount Windows: Access Windows partitions directly inside Android.
  • check_circle Backup & Restore: Backup critical partitions and restore them safely.
  • check_circle UEFI Manager: Flash and update UEFI images for Windows on ARM.
  • check_circle Quick Switch: Reboot to Windows instantly via App or Homescreen Widget.
  • check_circle Optimization: Install ReviOS/AtlasOS Playbooks & Frameworks.
  • check_circle Toolbox: Insert variety of WOA scripts & Switch to Android (STA) tools.
